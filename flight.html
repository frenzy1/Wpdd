Fly to Sydney

Sydney is the capital of New South Wales and is home to the world famous Opera house and Harbour bridge. Sydney is also home to some of the popular destinations like Bondi Beach and Taronga Zoo.

Fun Facts about Sydney:

** Sydney used to be called Albion until it was renamed after British Lord Sydney.

** Sydney has the largest population in Australia with about 5 million people as of 2017.

** The Sydney Fish Market is the largest market of its kind in the Southern Hemisphere and the world's 3rd largest fish market.

Fly to Melbourne

Melbourne is the coastal capital of Victoria, the south eastern territory of Australia. It is famous for its heritage buildings like its Parliament house and Victoria library. It is home to other famous destinations like the Great Ocean Road and Phillip Island.

Fun Facts about Melbourne:

** Melbourne’s tramway system is the largest outside Europe and the fourth largest in the world, stretching along 244km of track and boasting 450 trams.

** Melbourne was originally going to be named ‘Batmania’ after one of its founding fathers, John Batman

** Melbourne was the capital city of Australia for 26 years between 1901 and 1927 before the capital became Canberra.

Fly to Brisbane

Brisbane is the capital of Queensland which is also known as the "Sunshine State", the north eastern territory of Australia. It is home to Gold Coast, famous for long sandy beaches and surfing spots .

Fun Facts about Melbourne:

** The first ever lamington was made here.

** Brisbane was the busiest submarine port in the world in WW2

** Brisbane is the third largest city in Australia
